About me/us

💻 ⚛️ Trujillo Research Group – Computational Organic Chemistry at the University of Manchester. We develop and apply computational strategies for catalyst design and mechanistic insight in organic and organocatalytic reactions.

⚛️ We combine quantum chemical calculations, data‑driven and machine‑learning models, and close collaboration with experimental partners to understand, predict, and ultimately control reactivity and selectivity in catalytic systems.

📚 For publications, team members, open positions and news, see our group website:
https://trujilloresearchgroup.com

🤝 We are always open to collaboration and code contributions. If you are interested in joining forces on computational catalysis, mechanistic studies, or ML for chemistry, feel free to get in touch or open an issue/pull request on any of our public repositories.
